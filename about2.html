THE BEGINNING OF THE ERA

The Handshake That Started It All

Phil Knight grew up in Southeast Portland and attended Cleveland High School. Early setbacks shaped his resilience — including being cut from his baseball team.

Encouraged by his mother, Knight chose track over disappointment. That single decision sparked a journey that would redefine athletics forever.

Though physically smaller, Knight was fast, disciplined, and fiercely competitive. His dedication soon paid off, earning recognition in Oregon’s elite prep scene.

A pivotal moment came when his father introduced him to legendary Oregon track coach Bill Bowerman. What began as a handshake would later become one of the most influential partnerships in sports history.

Inspired by Bowerman’s athletes and philosophy, Knight realized that sport could be transformed through innovation, belief, and relentless effort.
